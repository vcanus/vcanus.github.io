## What is MLOps?

**MLOps (Machine Learning Operations)** combines **machine learning, data engineering, and IT operations** to automate and optimize the **entire ML lifecycle**‚Äîfrom data collection and model training to deployment and continuous monitoring. It accelerates model development, ensures operational reliability, and enables scalable, seamless deployment in both **industrial and general applications**.

TSLoom provides **comprehensive end-to-end MLOps capabilities**, making it easier to operationalize and scale ML solutions‚Äîwhether for **factories, smart cities, or custom applications**.
